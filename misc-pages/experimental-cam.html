OVERVIEW

what is it?

an interactive scent-based installation that invites users to write a letter to a scent curated based on letters written to myself from 2016-2025

intent

for my senior year thesis project, i aimed to reimagine portraiture through the lens of scent and explore the iterative nature of identity that can manifest in the form of letters.
